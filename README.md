## Project Overview:
Fly Easy is a flight booking interface designed to simplify the user experience by reducing overwhelming options and increasing pricing transparency. Our goal is to help users quickly find and confidently book flights.

---

## User Research Summary

Our user research focused on understanding how users interact with flight-booking platforms and identifying usability issues. We tested three participants (ages 20–22) with different levels of experience booking flights.
We used a think-aloud method, where users explained their thoughts while completing tasks.

### Tasks Tested
- Simple: Enter travel details and view flights  
- Medium: Review flight details  
- Complex: Use advanced search and complete booking 

### Key Findings
- Users prefer **fewer, curated flight options**
- Simple layouts reduce confusion
- **Upfront pricing builds trust**
- Users dislike hidden fees
- Mobile design felt more intuitive

## Storyboard

### User Group
College students

### Scenario
Alex, an FSU student, is booking a flight home for spring break on his phone.

### Flow
1. Alex realizes he needs a flight and opens the app  
2. He enters travel details  
3. Reviews 3 recommended flights  
4. Selects one and checks details  
5. Chooses a seat  
6. Enters payment info  
7. Confirms booking
